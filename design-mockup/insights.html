1

Bullish Put Spreads

Credit Strategy
GOOD

Entry Criteria

  • IV Rank: 30-50 (adequate premium collection)
  • Trend: Uptrend or range-bound confirmation required
  • Support: 3%+ above key support levels
  • DTE: 40-50 days for optimal theta decay
  • Sector Preference: Defensive sectors in current regime

Position Specifications

  • Strike Selection: Short put 3-5% OTM
  • Spread Width: $5 for high-priced stocks, $2-3 for mid-cap
  • Position Size: 3-5% of portfolio per position
  • Max Positions: 8-10 open positions across sectors

Exit Rules

  • Profit Target: 50-70% of max profit
  • Stop Loss: 2x credit received (200% loss)
  • Time Exit: Close by 21 DTE regardless
  • Technical Exit: If support breaks, close immediately

Why This Works in Current Environment

Normal volatility (VIX 19.08) provides adequate premium while maintaining manageable risk profiles. Defensive sector strength supports bullish put positions.

Neutral Iron Condors

Credit Strategy
IDEAL

Entry Criteria

  • IV Rank: 15-40 (low to fair premium optimal)
  • Trend: Range-bound, no strong directional bias
  • Range Width: Support/Resistance 5%+ apart
  • DTE: 45 days (sweet spot for theta)
  • VIX Rule: Close if VIX > 20 (regime change)

Position Specifications

  • Strike Selection: Wings 7-10% OTM each side
  • Spread Width: $5 wings preferred for liquidity
  • Position Size: 5-8% of portfolio (both sides combined)
  • Balance: Equal credit from call and put sides

Exit Rules

  • Profit Target: 50% of max profit (aggressive management)
  • Stop Loss: 2x credit received or side tested
  • Time Exit: 21 DTE close mandatory
  • VIX Rule: Close all ICs if VIX breaks > 20
  • Side Management: Close tested side, let winner run

⭐ Why This Is IDEAL Right Now

VIX at 19.08 creates perfect range-bound conditions. Low volatility environment allows theta decay to work optimally while maintaining controlled risk. This is the systematic "sweet spot" for iron condors.
